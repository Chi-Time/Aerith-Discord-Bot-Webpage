Say Hi to Aerith Bot!

Aerith Bot is a user-created Discord bot for the Discord application. She is created within the C# language using the unofficial Discord.NET API.

She is designed to help interact with others, provide entertainment, decisions and games. You can explore her various functionality at the following wiki: documentation

Aerith's History

Aerith bot is created by a single person. She was originally developed and designed for work on a small server with my friends. But, since development has gone so well, I hope that she can be used for many others. Whilst I have no professional experience in the realms of software development, I hope the bot can be of use.
